Sambar

Description

Today we'll make tasty sambar for dosa and pongal

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp oil
  • ½ tsp mustard
  • ½ tsp jeera
  • 6 shallots/1 big onion
  • Few curry leaves
  • ¼ tsp hing
  • 1 tomato
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp sambar powder
  • ½ tsp salt (as per taste)
  • 2 cups water
  • Few coriander leaves
  • ½ cup toor dal
  • 1 carrot or any sambar veg

Steps

  1. Wash and soak toor dal in enough water for 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Pressure- cook the dal for 3 to 4 whistles until soft.
  3. Mash and keep it aside.
  4. In a kadai, heat oil, add mustard and jeera seeds.
  5. When mustard starts spluttering add onions and curry leaves. Add asafoetida.
  6. While onions turn transparent, add chopped tomato and cook until soft.
  7. Add turmeric powder and sambar powder.
  8. Now add the chopped vegetable with some water to cook.
  9. Let the vegetable cook for 5 minutes with the masala.
  10. Open the lid and add mashed toor dal. Mix Dal with the cooked vegetable.
  11. Now add required salt for the Sambar.
  12. Pour a cup of water to make a thin consistency.
  13. Close with a lid and let the Sambar boil for five more minutes.
  14. Finally, garnish with coriander leaves.
  15. Switch off and serve hot with Pongal or dosa.
